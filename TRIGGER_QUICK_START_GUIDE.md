# Trigger System Quick Start Guide

## What are Triggers?

Triggers are keywords that modify how the AI responds to your requests. They help you get more specific, targeted, and useful responses.

## New: 250+ Triggers in 9 Categories

The system now includes comprehensive triggers for:
1. Reasoning & Analysis
2. Research & Information
3. Planning & Organization
4. Communication & Style
5. Coding & Development
6. Creative & Writing
7. Data & Analytics
8. Business & Strategy
9. Education & Learning

## Quick Examples

### "I need help reasoning through this problem"
Uses the `reason` trigger to provide step-by-step logical thinking.

### "Analyze the market and do a competitor analysis"
Combines `analyze` + `competitor_analysis` for comprehensive market insight.

### "Write a poem with sensory details"
Uses `poem` + `sensory` triggers for creative output.

### "Debug this code and review for security"
Combines `debug` + `security_review` for thorough code analysis.

### "Create a learning path with exercises"
Uses `learning_path` + `practice_exercise` for educational content.

## 9 Major Categories Explained

### 1. Reasoning & Analysis
**Best for**: Thinking through problems, analyzing arguments, evaluating options
**Triggers**: reason, analyze, debate, compare, evaluate, troubleshoot
**Example**: "Reason through this decision"

### 2. Research & Information
**Best for**: Finding facts, summarizing content, verifying information
**Triggers**: search, deep_research, fact_check, summarize, define, clarify
**Example**: "Deep research this topic and summarize"

### 3. Planning & Organization
**Best for**: Project planning, creating workflows, organizing ideas
**Triggers**: plan, roadmap, checklist, prioritize, organize, brainstorm
**Example**: "Create a roadmap with milestones"

### 4. Communication & Style
**Best for**: Adjusting tone, targeting audience, formatting content
**Triggers**: simplify, formalize, persuasive, informative, neutral, casual
**Example**: "Simplify this for a 10-year-old"

### 5. Coding & Development
**Best for**: Programming tasks, code quality, architecture
**Triggers**: code, debug, refactor, optimize, test, security_review
**Example**: "Write optimized code with unit tests"

### 6. Creative & Writing
**Best for**: Stories, poems, creative content, editing

**Example**: "Write a descriptive paragraph with sensory details"

### 7. Data & Analytics
**Best for**: Data analysis, visualization, statistics, predictions

### 8. Business & Strategy
**Best for**: Strategy, market analysis, business planning

**Best for**: Teaching, learning design, assessment

## How to Use

### Method 1: Type Trigger Keywords
```
"Please analyze and critique this proposal"
```

### Method 2: Use Trigger Gallery
1. Open ChatArea
2. Click "Trigger Gallery" button
3. Search or browse triggers
4. Select triggers
5. Send message

### Method 3: Combine Triggers
```
"Create a learning_objective, concept_explanation, and practice_exercise for this topic"
```
