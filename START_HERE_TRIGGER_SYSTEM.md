## ðŸ’¡ Key Concepts (1-Minute Explanation)

### System Prompts
> When a user types "reason through this", the system sends an enhanced prompt to the AI that says: "Structure your response with <reason> tags, provide detailed thinking, ensure comprehensive analysis."

### Memory Variables
> The system tracks what triggers you use and generates: "User employed triggers: reason, analyze". This is sent to the AI internally to help it understand your preferences.

### Smart Visibility
> Custom triggers (ones you create) show bars in the chat. Built-in triggers work in the background without showing bars. Keeps the interface clean.

### Long-Form Responses
> With triggers active, the AI is guided to provide longer, more detailed responses with structured thinking sections instead of brief answers.
